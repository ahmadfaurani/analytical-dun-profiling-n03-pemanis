### 2.2 Turnout Sensitivity Modelling

**Historical Pattern:**
- **Turnout >80%:** PH competitive (2018: won by 363 votes)
- **Turnout 60-70%:** BN favored but contestable
- **Turnout <60%:** BN safe (2022: won by 4,187 votes)

**Mathematical Relationship:**
```
PH Vote ≈ (Turnout % − 55) × 500 votes per percentage point

  PH ≈ (84 − 55) × 500 = 14,500 × 0.57 ≈ 8,300 ✓

At 59% turnout (2022):
  PH ≈ (59 − 55) × 500 = 2,000 (underestimates; actual ~4,141)

```
